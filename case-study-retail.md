# Case Study: E-commerce Customer Retention

## The Problem:
A retail company (like Jumia) noticed that 40% of new customers buy one item and never return to the website.

## The Goal:
Identify "Why" they aren't coming back and suggest a data-driven way to increase repeat purchases.

## Data Needed for Analysis:
- Customer feedback/rating after the first purchase
- Delivery times compared to promised dates
- Product accuracy (matching details provided)

## Potential Insights:
If the data shows that **late deliveries** are the main reason for churn, the business should offer a discount code for the next order.
